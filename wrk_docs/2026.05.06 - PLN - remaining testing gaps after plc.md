## Recommended Next Slice

Do Phase 3.1 as a narrow DRED/DNN coverage-contract slice, not as "make all DRED parity pass".

```text

```

Why this next:

- It removes another major source of false confidence: neural/DRED tests can look present while being ignored, skipped for missing weights, or pinned to unimplemented stubs.
- It is closer to the original plan's "green means precise" goal than performance or mutation work.
- It is smaller and more controllable than real-world corpus curation if scoped to claim policy, preflight, and oracle labeling first.
- It gives future DRED implementation work a clean test contract before anyone starts touching fragile DNN/FARGAN/LPCNet math.
