## Observations

- Debug is ~87 s; release is ~147 s (~1.7× slower than debug due to codegen optimisations over 36 k lines of fixed-point arithmetic).
- The i7-7820HQ is saturated at 8 threads; most of the wall time goes to LLVM codegen in parallel. Incremental builds after a single-file change are sub-second for debug, a few seconds for release.
- Disk footprint is modest: debug stores full debuginfo, release is leaner at 0.52 GB.
- A modern 8-core/16-thread machine (e.g. i9-13900H) would likely cut cold release time to ~50–60 s based on linear scaling of the codegen stage.
