lane (quality, tests+coverage, ambisonics, bench) and emits an HTML report at
`tests/results/full_test_<YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS>.html`. See HLD for full spec.

Each build phase follows the pattern:
1. **Implement** (general-purpose agent, TDD where it fits).
2. **Code review** (code-reviewer skill) **+** **Devils-advocate** (challenger) — in parallel.
3. **Apply feedback** (code-review-apply-changes skill).
4. **Supervisor commits.**
