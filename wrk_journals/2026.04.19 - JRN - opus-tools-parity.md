  `MAX_PACKET_BYTES = 1275` (= `MAX_OPUS_FRAME_BYTES * 1`) silently

  `MAX_PACKET_BYTES = 7650`. Regression test added
  (`encode_framesize_120_hard_cbr_128k_fills_packet_buffer`) —
  proves we're within ±10% of the 128k × 5s expected size.
