## Bugs Found and Fixed This Session

### SILK Encoder (3 fixes)
1. **silk_control_snr rate**: Used raw `enc_control.bit_rate` instead of adjusted `target_rate`
2. **NSQ k==2 shift**: Delayed-decision reset used `gains_q16[1]>>6` shift 8 instead of shift 14
3. **NSQ pulse indexing** (CRITICAL): Used `subfr*length+i-decision_delay` but `subfr` resets
   to 0 at k=2 for voiced frames. C uses pointer arithmetic that auto-advances. Fixed by
   adding `pulse_base_offset = k * subfr_length` parameter.

### CELT Decoder (3 fixes)
4. **B<<=recombine**: Missing restore of B after recombine undo in quant_band resynthesis.
   Collapse mask truncated to 4 bits instead of 8. **MAJOR**: Reduced roundtrip max_diff
   from 23044→8 for sine, noise@128k to max_diff=1.
5. **Lowband scratch copy**: Removed `lowband_scratch.is_some()` gate — always copy when
   transforms needed. Fixes Haar transform corruption.
6. **Saturation range**: N+overlap → N in celt_synthesis, matching C reference.

### Opus Encoder (2 fixes)
7. **dc_reject symmetry**: `sat16()` clamps to [-32768,32767] but C's SATURATE clamps to
   [-32767,32767]. Fixed for loud signals near clipping.
8. **Bandwidth interpolation**: Quadratic formula instead of hard threshold (correct but
   stereo thresholds currently identical, no test impact).
