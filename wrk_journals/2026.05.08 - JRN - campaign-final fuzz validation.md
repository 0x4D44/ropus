**Diagnosis (data-only — no codec change proposed).** The post-fix corpus is heavily seeded with
8 kHz inputs:

| sample_rate | Frames |
|------------:|-------:|
|       8 000 |  4 853 |
|      16 000 |     88 |

`set_force_mode(MODE_HYBRID)` was actually requested 221 times in the probe. Every one of those
221 requests came in at sr=8 000, where HYBRID is invalid (HYBRID requires WB+SWB which means
≥ 16 kHz internal SILK + CELT high-band). The encoder silently falls back to SILK_ONLY in that
case, which is why the HYBRID column reads zero. This is corpus-bias rather than encoder-fault —
`forced=1001 → mode=1000` 221/221 times — and matches the documented HYBRID admissibility rules.

Implication for the HLD floors: neither the 30 % flip nor the 5 % SILK↔HYBRID bar can be met until
the seed/grammar bias is rebalanced toward 24/48 kHz inputs. **Not in scope for Stream G2 / this
validation pass — flagged for follow-up.** No artifacts produced; the differential check itself
remained green throughout.
