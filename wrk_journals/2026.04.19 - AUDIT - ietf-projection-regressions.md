### G2 — `test_24bit_decode_plc_matches_c_reference` still `#[ignore]`'d (SCOPE, non-blocking)

**What:** Full-frame PLC decode24 test at `harness/tests/c_ref_differential.rs:1628` remains `#[ignore]`'d. The 60c6a97 SILK PLC fix resolved the bulk of the divergence — first 288 samples now bit-exact (enforced by `test_silk_plc_first_subframes_match_c_reference`) — but residual LSB-level drift accumulates past that point. No IETF vector currently exercises long-enough lost-packet sequences to surface this.

**Fix:** Not required for HLD closeout. The residual drift is a follow-up bug, distinct from the HLD's deliverables. Journal lines 562-567 explicitly time-box this for follow-up.

**Severity:** not-blocking. Noted for context; HLD doesn't promise bit-exact full-frame PLC — only that the `ietf_vectors` binary (which uses PLC on zero-length packets) exits 0, which it does.

### G3 — 4 of 6 `ropus/src/opus/multistream.rs` getters are dead code (CODE HYGIENE, non-blocking, pre-flagged)

Only `nb_channels()` on both is consumed by `capi/src/projection.rs`. The 4 `nb_streams`/`nb_coupled_streams` variants are never called. Pre-flagged in `2026.04.19 - DA - piece-b.md` Challenge 2.

## Over-deliveries (beyond HLD scope)

1. **`opus_encode24` capi entry point** (A): HLD didn't scope, but `opus_demo.c`'s encode arm link-references it. Shipped as `OPUS_UNIMPLEMENTED` stub after DA review (initial commit had a real implementation that DA flagged as untested+diverging-from-C on overflow edges).
2. **DRED link-stubs** (A, `tests/conformance/src/dred_stub.c`): 7 symbols. HLD didn't anticipate this; required because `opus_demo.c` references `opus_dred_*` unconditionally even though the guarded code paths are dead on valid bitstreams.
3. **Decoder-bug fixes + regression tests** (surfaced by Piece A): two genuine Rust decoder bugs (SILK mono→stereo resampler state, SILK PLC subfr geometry + LTP bias). HLD anticipated "the IETF suite will surface bugs" and the team took the time to fix them in-scope rather than defer.
4. **`opus_projection_encode_float` real implementation** (C): HLD was silent; Piece B left it `OPUS_UNIMPLEMENTED`; Piece C replaced it with a saturating float→i16 FFI shim when `projection_overflow2/3` regressions required it. Documented deviation from C-reference ordering.
5. **`float_cast.h` + `mathops.h` stubs + `arch.h` expansion** (B): required for `mapping_matrix.c` to compile under MSVC without dragging `reference/celt/` onto the include path.
6. **6 getter methods on projection types** (B, ropus): 34 LOC of one-line delegations; 4 of 6 unused (G3). Harmless but speculative.

None of these are problematic; items 3 and 4 are genuine value-adds.
