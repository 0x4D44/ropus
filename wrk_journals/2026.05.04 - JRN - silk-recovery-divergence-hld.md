## Design notes

Wrote `/home/md/language/ropus/wrk_docs/2026.05.04 - HLD - silk-recovery-divergence.md`.

Core design decision in the HLD: do not call the future gate merely "well formed." The useful property is "SNR-comparable coded audio." A zero-length DTX/recovery sub-frame may be structurally accepted while still producing concealment PCM that is not a fair SNR target.

Frustration: the existing `snr_oracle_applicable_for_packet(reference, packet_well_formed)` name nudges future work toward the wrong mental model. The fix should probably introduce a stronger classifier name rather than keep threading booleans.
