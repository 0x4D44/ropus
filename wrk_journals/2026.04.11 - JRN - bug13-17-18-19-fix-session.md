## Pre-Investigation Analysis (Supervisor)

### Bug #17 — CNG buffer memmove
**C** (`CNG.c:115-116`): Shifts buffer right by `subfr_length` via `silk_memmove`, then copies new excitation into first `subfr_length` slots. Buffer acts as FIFO of `nb_subfr` subframes.
**Rust** (`decoder.rs:1402-1408`): Only copies new excitation into `[0..subfr_length]`, overwriting without shift. History lost.

### Bug #18 — CNG excitation mask
**C** (`CNG.c:46-49`): `exc_mask = CNG_BUF_MASK_MAX; while(exc_mask > length) exc_mask >>= 1;` — power-of-2 mask ≤ `length`.
**Rust** (`decoder.rs:1436`): `CNG_BUF_MASK_MAX.min(subfr_length - 1)` — wrong variable (`subfr_length` not `length`) and no power-of-2 halving.

### Bug #19 — Stereo pred_Q13 type
**C** (`stereo_decode_pred.c:37`): `opus_int32 pred_Q13[]` — i32 throughout decode path.
**C** (`dec_API.c:154`): `opus_int32 MS_pred_Q13[2]` — local is i32.
**C** (`structs.h:124`): `pred_prev_Q13` is `opus_int16[2]` — truncation only at storage.
**Rust** (`decoder.rs:1484`): `pred_q13: &mut [i16; 2]` — truncation at decode, too early.

### Bug #13 — WB decode divergence
Most complex. Prior 6+ hour investigation agents couldn't identify root cause. Affects LPC order 16 only. Divergence appears in 2nd sub-frame. Dynamic tracing recommended.
