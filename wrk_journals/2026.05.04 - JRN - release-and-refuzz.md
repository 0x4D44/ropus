## 2026-05-05 00:06 BST

Investigated the DNN warning before publishing.

Findings:

- `ropus/build.rs` intentionally emits an empty compile-time blob when `reference/dnn` is absent.
- `ropus/Cargo.toml` packages only the crate-local source/build files, not the workspace-level `reference/` tree.
- `ropus/README.md` documents this for crates.io installs: callers can provide weights through `Decoder::set_dnn_blob` or `OpusDecoder::set_dnn_blob`.

Decision: proceed with publish, treating the warning as a documented caveat rather than a blocker. The caveat is still material: published `ropus` does not include out-of-the-box neural PLC weights.

Emotional temperature: still cautious, but the warning is now understood instead of mysterious.
