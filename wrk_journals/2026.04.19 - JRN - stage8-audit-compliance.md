## Overall verdict

**Stage 8 is substantively complete per the HLD's intent.** 9 of 10 HLD rows Ō£ģ, one ŌÜĀ’ĖÅ (Stage 8.0 harness re-scoped per Arthur's Option D; documented), and one ŌÜĀ’ĖÅ (Stage 8.6's WAV acceptance gated on upstream LPCNet drift, correctly escalated). **What's genuinely outstanding that could reasonably block closure:**

- **G2 (Stage 8.6 ignored test).** If "Stage 8 complete" requires the WAV byte-exact oracle to flip from `#[ignore]` to green, Stage 8 is blocked on Stage 7's LPCNet bit-exactness work ŌĆö not on any Stage-8-authored code. The 8.1/8.2/8.4/8.5/8.7 oracles already prove the DRED code correct; fixing LPCNet will cause 8.6's test to pass without DRED-side changes (per 8.6 journal's layered diagnostics).

**What's not material:**

- G1 is a documentation-vs-implementation delta with sound rationale.
- G3/G4 are low-bitrate/DTX edge-case fidelity gaps that HLD explicitly scoped out of 8.8's "format-level cross-compat" exit.
- G5 is a trivial CLI-flag omission; integration validation exists via harness-deep-plc.
- G6 is a placement choice that the HLD leaves open to Stage 7's joint PR.

**Recommendation.** Land 8.10 as a close-out commit citing G2 as the one open escalation, hand off CTL-dispatch wiring + FARGAN seam body to Stage 7 per HLD ┬¦Stage 7 coordination, and move on.
