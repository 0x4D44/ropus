## Cross-cutting findings

- **HLD v1ŌåÆv2 transition was handled cleanly.** The HLD explicitly
  records that v1 was superseded; journal entries reference v2
  consistently. No stale acceptance text is invoked by Stage 6/7
  journals.
- **Tier transitions documented.** Every tier-1 ŌåÆ tier-2 drop (Stages
  6.4c, 7b.3) includes measurement evidence, not assertion. In 7b.3's
  case the evidence is a dedicated control experiment rather than
  a post-hoc justification ŌĆö the right instinct.
- **No silent `#[ignore]` of tier-1 tests.** Ignored tests in
  `c_ref_differential.rs` are diagnostic helpers (6 total) and the
  24-bit PLC line (explicitly pending); none are tier-1 acceptance
  tests that were silently weakened to hide a failure.
- **Weights blob sharing works correctly across Stages 7-8.**
  `$OUT_DIR/weights_blob.bin` is the single mechanism;
  `ropus/build/gen_weights_blob.c` gates DRED arrays on
  `ROPUS_HAVE_DRED_WEIGHTS`. Blob size (8,785,408) pinned in
  `ropus/src/opus/decoder.rs:3236`.
- **HLD's own tier-2 amendment has the right flavor.** A test that
  would pass only if f32 libm rounding were deterministic across
  libc versions is a test of something other than concealment
  correctness ŌĆö measuring the C-vs-C ceiling and gating above it is
  the honest calibration.
- **DNN CTL exposure in `capi/`.** `OPUS_SET_DNN_BLOB` is reachable
  via `mdopus_decoder_ctl_set_dnn_blob` (pointer dispatcher) and the
  variadic shim at `capi/src/ctl_shim.c:290`. `OPUS_GET_DNN_BLOB` is
  NOT exposed ŌĆö matches xiph upstream policy
  (`tests/conformance/include/opus_defines.h:175` carries the
  commented-out `/*#define OPUS_GET_DNN_BLOB_REQUEST 4053 */`).
