### Findings + resolution

1. **Pause-glyph cell-width mismatch** (medium). `⏸ U+23F8` is
   Emoji-classified and renders as 2 display cells on Windows Terminal;
   `chars().count()` returns 1. Truncation budget uses `chars().count()`,
   so the paused line got a 1-char-too-generous title budget and could
   wrap on narrow terminals. Exactly the flicker the HLD tried to avoid.

   **Applier-B** (`ae598cae54bf394e3`) — first pass padded the play
   glyph `"▶ "` to equalize visual rendering (3 cells each). Visually
   state-invariant, but the char-count budget math remained off by 1 in
   pause state. Redirected via SendMessage: swap pause glyph to
   `‖ U+2016 DOUBLE VERTICAL LINE` (cell-width 1, `chars().count()` == 1
   cells). Fix applied: 1-line glyph change + 1-line test update.
   Budget math now correct for both states; no new deps, no char-vs-str
   gymnastics. Final: 65 pass, clean build, zero clippy on `play.rs`.

2. **`#[allow(dead_code)]` × 4 on staging helpers** (low). Devil flagged
   the forget-risk. Accepted for now per the staging discipline; stage 3
   wires all four helpers in and removes the allows. The supervisor
   journal (this document) + task list are the forget-prevention
   mechanism.
