## Key Insight

The pattern is mechanical: run mutation testing → identify survivors → compute exact
output values → assert_eq!. The hard part is distinguishing equivalent mutants from
real gaps. Roughly 20% of survivors in silk/common.rs were provably equivalent
(same output for all inputs due to boundary coincidences in lookup tables).
